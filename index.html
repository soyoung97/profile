Research Interest

I am a PhD student at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. Seungwon Hwang. I want to develop NLP systems that are beneficial and informative to people in everyday life. To achieve this, I am focusing on endowing language models with the ability to:
(1) efficiently stay up-to-date with new knowledge and human feedback,
(2) Incorporate internal (commonsense) knowledge with external knowledge,
(3) Generate reliable responses grounded on external knowledge base.
